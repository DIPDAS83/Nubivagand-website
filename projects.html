Astro Bangla

Astro Bangla is one of Nubivagant Technology’s flagship projects—an innovative digital platform that brings traditional astrology into the modern era. Designed with Bengali users in mind, Astro Bangla offers personalized horoscope readings, daily predictions, and astrological consultations in an easy-to-use app and website format. Built with a sleek UI and robust backend, the platform supports real-time chart generation, birth chart analysis, and matching services, all powered by advanced algorithms and Vedic astrology principles. It’s available in both English and Bengali to ensure cultural relevance and accessibility. Astro Bangla reflects Nubivagant’s commitment to blending heritage with technology for everyday use.

Progressive

Progressive Edu is an educational platform developed by Nubivagant Technology with the vision to make learning accessible, engaging, and effective for students of all levels. It serves as a digital bridge between students and quality education, offering online courses, mock tests, video lectures, and personalized learning paths. Tailored for both school-level learners and competitive exam aspirants, Progressive Edu is built with a user-friendly interface and smart analytics to track student progress and performance. Teachers and institutions can also use the platform to manage classes, upload content, and interact with students in real time. Progressive Edu reflects Nubivagant’s mission to transform education through innovation.

Plant Nursery Kolkata

Plant Nursery Kolkata is an e-commerce platform developed by Nubivagant Technology, designed to cater to the growing demand for gardening enthusiasts in Kolkata. The platform offers a wide variety of plants, pots, soil, tools, fertilizers, and pesticides, providing a comprehensive gardening solution to customers. With a user-friendly interface, customers can browse through various collections, including live plants, garden accessories, and gifting solutions. The platform also offers home delivery services within Kolkata, ensuring that customers receive their gardening essentials at their doorstep. Additionally, expert support is available to assist customers with plant care and maintenance, enhancing their gardening experience.
